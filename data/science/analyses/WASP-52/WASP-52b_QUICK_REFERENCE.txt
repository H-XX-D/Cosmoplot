CHALLENGES & OPPORTUNITIES
================================================================================
Challenges:
  - Fainter star reduces transmission depth by ~factor of 2
  - K-dwarf UV production uncertain; affects photochemistry predictions
  - Multiple molecular species expected; complex spectral deconvolution
  - Thermal emission dominated by planet's internal heat

Opportunities:
  - Ideal for detecting multiple molecular species simultaneously
  - K-dwarf coupling allows testing host star effects
  - Water detection more likely than in ultra-hots
  - Possible methane detection would be novel for hot Jupiter
  - Lower temperature allows testing of chemical models at intermediate regime
