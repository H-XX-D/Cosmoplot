## Summary

HAT-P-12b is the most inflated exoplanet known, with an enormous H/He envelope and minimal core. Warm dayside temperature (900 K) combined with extreme atmospheric extension create the most spectacular transmission spectrum of any exoplanet. Water and methane both reach 300-600 ppm in transmission, making this world perhaps the single best target for JWST transmission spectroscopy to characterize molecular abundances and atmospheric dynamics.
