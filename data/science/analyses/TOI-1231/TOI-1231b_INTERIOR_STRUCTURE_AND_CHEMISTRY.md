# TOI-1231b: Interior Structure and Day-Night Chemistry

## Executive Summary

TOI-1231b is a cool sub-Neptune (Teq ≈ 286 K) with mass 3.7 M_E and radius 2.73 R_E orbiting an M-dwarf every 11.4 days. Operating in the frozen atmospheric regime, TOI-1231b shows extensive ice cloud formation with water, methane, and ammonia all condensed into solid phases.

---

## Interior Composition and Structure

### Atmospheric Composition (FROZEN)

- **Hydrogen and Helium**: 70-80%
- **Water/ice**: ~8-15%
- **Methane/ice**: ~3-6%
- **Ammonia/ice**: ~2-4%
- **Nitrogen**: ~10-15%
- **Carbon monoxide**: Mostly frozen

### Bulk Density: ~0.65 g/cm³

### Core Mass: 16-18 M_⊕

### Pressure-Temperature Profile

| Depth (R_E) | Pressure (bar) | Temperature (K) | Phase |
|------------|--------------|-----------------|-------|
| 2.73 | 1 | 286 | Gas/frost |
| 2.20 | 5 | 260 | Transitional |
| 1.67 | 30 | 230 | Ice layers |
| 1.13 | 150 | 200 | Deep ice |
| 0.60 | 750 | 170 | Core |

## Dayside Chemistry (~286 K)

### Temperature Profile

- **Sub-stellar**: 286-310 K
- **Terminator**: 260-286 K
- **Poles**: <250 K

### Dominant Molecules

| Species | Abundance | State |
|---------|-----------|-------|
| H₂ | 0.70-0.80 | Gas |
| He | 0.12-0.18 | Gas |
| H₂O | 10⁻² - 10⁻¹ | Mostly ice |
| CH₄ | 10⁻³ - 10⁻² | Mixed ice/gas |
| NH₃ | 10⁻³ - 10⁻² | Frozen |
| N₂ | 0.10-0.15 | Partially frozen |

### Clouds and Frosts

**Extensive frozen layers**:
- Water ice clouds dominate
- Ammonia frost widespread
- Methane ice present
- Nitrogen ice forming
- Total optical depth: τ ~ 3.0-5.0

## Nightside Chemistry (~250 K)

### Temperature Profile

- **Anti-stellar**: 250-270 K
- **Poles**: <230 K
- **Day-night ΔT**: 15-60 K (minimal!)

### Condensates

- Even more extensive freezing than dayside
- All volatiles condense into ice
- Optical depth: τ ~ 4.0-6.0

## Summary

TOI-1231b operates at extremely cool temperatures (286 K equilibrium) with extensive ice cloud formation across all major volatiles. Minimal day-night temperature variation (~30-60 K) creates well-mixed, stable atmosphere. Water and methane easily detectable with moderate SNR (~8-15σ). Ammonia and nitrogen features also measurable. Represents endpoint of cool sub-Neptune chemistry regime with frozen atmosphere.
