# TOI-3757b: Interior Structure and Day-Night Chemistry

## Executive Summary

TOI-3757b is a warm hot Jupiter (Teq ≈ 1000 K) with mass 0.39 M_J and radius 1.11 R_J orbiting an F-star every 3.13 days. Moderately dense for warm regime, TOI-3757b shows balanced molecular chemistry with strong water and methane features.

## Summary

TOI-3757b is a warm hot Jupiter with balanced molecular chemistry. Dayside temperature (1000 K) enables coexistence of water, methane, and ammonia. Moderate density suggests classical hot Jupiter interior structure. JWST observations will detect multiple molecular species across infrared wavelengths.
