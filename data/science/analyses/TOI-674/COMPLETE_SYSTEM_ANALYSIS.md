- CO: 5-20 ppm (photochemical product)
- SO₂: 0.01-0.1% (temperature-dependent, marginal)
- CO₂: 0.1-1 ppm (oxidation product)

### M-Dwarf Photochemistry at 635 K

At 635 K, TOI-674b's photochemistry is MODERATELY ACTIVE:

**Water Channel (Primary):**
- H₂O + hν → H + OH (moderate photolysis rate)
- OH + H₂ → H₂O + H (recycling regenerates H₂O)
- Result: H₂O abundant (1-3%), cloud-forming

**Methane Channel (Secondary):**
- CH₄ + OH → CH₃ + H₂O (fast at 635 K)
- CH₃ + HO₂ → CH₄ + O₂ (regeneration)
- Result: CH₄ elevated to 0.2-0.5% (M-dwarf enhanced)

**Sulfur Channel (Marginal):**
- H₂S + OH → SH + H₂O (slower than at 759 K)
- SO₂ production: Slow but possible
- Result: SO₂ at 0.01-0.1% (detectable but weak)

### M-dwarf UV photochemistry at 635 K

**Water-Hydroxyl Cycle (Enhanced):**
```
H₂O + hν(EUV) → H + OH [Moderate dissociation]
OH + H₂ → H₂O + H [Efficient recycling]

Result: H₂O maintained at 1-3%, CH₄ oxidized but regenerated

Result: CH₄ lifetime extended, abundance 0.2-0.5%
```
